14.3. Upgrading BDR 0.9.x to 1.0

Users of PostgreSQL 0.9.x should upgrade to 1.0 before upgrading to 2.0. A direct upgrade from 0.9.x to 2.0 should work but is not tested as part of the BDR QA process.

For upgrade instructions see the 1.0 manual.
